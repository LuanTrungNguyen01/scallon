About

Scallon

In 1993 Paul Scallon acted on his passion to custom build homes. More than 25 years later, Scallon Custom Homes continues to build exceptional homes that meet the exact specifications of their customers. With a talented workforce, Scallon Custom Homes builds 20–25 homes per year, always paying close attention to detail and never sacrificing quality.

Scallon Custom Homes has proven their dedication to ensuring each client has a pleasant experience and projects run smoothly, on time and within budget. It’s no surprise that Scallon Custom Homes has received numerous Home Building Awards through the years and most recently received the 2018 Award of Excellence in Home Building.

From participating in the initial design to creating ideas that capture the essence of the homeowner’s lifestyle, Scallon Custom Homes is committed to bringing dream homes to life.Quality is not a question when building a Scallon Custom Home—it is rather, a guarantee. Concentrating on highly desirable neighborhoods close to schools, shopping and conveniences, Scallon Custom Homes enhances beautiful lots in Cedar Rapids, Robins, Marion, North Liberty, and Tiffin with lovely homes. These homes appear in a variety of price ranges based on the overall size and amenities a buyer chooses. If the current inventory of building sites doesn’t fit your needs, Scallon Custom Homes will custom build on your lot or assist you in finding the perfect space to call home.
